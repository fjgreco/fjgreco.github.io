BIO

Frank Greco is an executive IT architect at IBM, focused on machine learning, AI, and analytics. He works with higher education institutions, the life-science/healthcare industry, and state and local governments at the intersection of business and technology. He has worn many hats, including programmer, project manager, and IT architecture consultant.

In addition to his work with IBM, Mr. Greco is also the founder and president of a small company that provides public record research services to the legal community.

Mr. Greco graduated from the University of Chicago with a degree in mathematics and earned a MS in computer science and mathematics from the University of Minnesota. Mr. Greco is an avid archer, is married and lives in the Chicago area, and maintains close professional and personal ties with his academic roots at UChicago.
